### Energy_and_Climate:

## ETT: Electricity Transformer Temperature

# ETTh1:
**Source:** [zhouhaoyi Github Repository](https://github.com/zhouhaoyi/ETDataset/tree/main/ETT-small)
**Description:** Contains hourly data from a power transformer in China, collected from July 2016 to July 2018. It's a key benchmark for predicting oil temperature to ensure operational safety.
**Specifications:** Includes 17,520 hourly records and 7 features (6 related to electrical load and 1 target).
**Target Variable:** `OT` (Oil Temperature).
**Use Case:** Widely used for long-sequence time-series forecasting.
